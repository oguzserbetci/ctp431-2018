Final term project: Music and Audio Computing CTP431 @ KAIST

An infinite loop of ambient with squashy balls.

only tested with Google Chrome

Technical details

The circle is a step sequencer, the closer a circle gets to the center of the ring, lower the note. But notes are out of sync. They meet at every 80 steps. The instrument is selected randomly from piano, bass and harp. The velocity and the duration of the sound is calculated from the size of the balls. Bigger the ball the less velocity and longer the duration for the note.

Used frameworks:

  • paper.js
  • Tone.js
  • underscore.js
  • bootstrap
