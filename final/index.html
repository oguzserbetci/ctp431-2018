Final term project: Music and Audio Computing CTP431 @ KAIST

An infinite loop of ambient.

The circle is a step sequencer, the closer a circle gets to the center of the ring, lower the note. But notes are out of sync, which gives the sequence a longer field. They meet at every 80 steps. The instrument is selected randomly.

Inspiration
